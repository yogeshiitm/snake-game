Play Snake

Instructions

The rules are simple:

  • Use arrow keys of your keyboard to change the direction of the snake.
  • Explore the arena without bumping into walls.
  • Collect all the food you see, and grow your snake as big as u can!

Note: The game is currently supported only on a desktop, as the snake has to be controlled by arrow keys of the keyboard. But soon I will add button controls for touch mobile/tablet as well.
